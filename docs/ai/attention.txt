================================================================================
ATTENTION MECHANISMS: SCALED DOT-PRODUCT & MULTI-HEAD
================================================================================

1. CORE CONCEPT
---------------
Attention allows the model to focus on specific parts of the input sequence 
when producing a specific part of the output. It calculates a "relevance score" 
between tokens.

2. SCALED DOT-PRODUCT ATTENTION
-------------------------------
The mechanism uses three primary vectors:
* Query (Q): What I am looking for.
* Key (K): What I have to offer.
* Value (V): The information I provide.

Formula: Attention(Q, K, V) = softmax((QK^T) / sqrt(d_k))V

3. MULTI-HEAD ATTENTION (MHA)
-----------------------------
Instead of performing one single attention function, MHA runs multiple 
"heads" in parallel. Each head learns to attend to different types of 
information (e.g., one head for grammar, one for coreference, one for intent).

4. TYPES OF ATTENTION
---------------------
* Self-Attention: Tokens in a sequence attend to other tokens in the same sequence.
* Cross-Attention: Decoder tokens attend to Encoder output tokens.
* Masked Attention: Prevents the model from "looking ahead" at future tokens 
  during training (common in GPT-style models).
